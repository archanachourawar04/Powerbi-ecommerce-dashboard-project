# Powerbi-ecommerce-dashboard-project
The main objective of this Power BI dashboard is to analyze and visualize Madhav Ecommerce sales performance across different dimensions such as state, product category, customer, and payment mode.

# It helps to :
It helps to:

1.Understand the total sales amount, profit, quantity, and average order value (AOV).

2.Identify the top-performing states, products, and customers contributing to revenue.

3.Track monthly profit trends to observe high and low performance periods.

4.Compare sales and quantity by product category and sub-category.

5.Analyze customer payment preferences such as UPI, credit card, COD, etc.

6.Provide a quick overview of business performance to support better decision-making.
